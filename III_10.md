$E$ банахово, $A \in \mathcal{L}(L_2([0, 1]), E) \implies$

$A^* \in \mathcal{L}(E^*, L_2([0, 1]))$, $C([0, 1]) \subseteq \text{Im } A^*$

$y \in \text{Ker } A \implies Ay = 0 \implies \forall f \in E^* \implies f(Ay) = 0 \implies A^* f(y) = 0 \implies$

$\forall g \in \text{Im } A^* \implies (g, y) = 0$. В силу непрерывности скалярного произведения

$\forall g \in \overline{[\text{Im } A^*]} \implies (g, y) = 0$. $\overline{[C([0, 1])]}_{L_2([0, 1])} = L_2([0, 1]) \implies \forall g \in L_2([0, 1]) \implies (g, y) = 0 \implies (y, y) = 0$

$\implies y = 0 \implies \text{Ker } A = \{0\}$
